working methods

Restore the plains

Holistic management is about how humans can live in a fragile ecosystem like the savanna or the big grass plains. The key is to mimic nature rather than cultivate the land. Discover the function of the grazers.

Plant a tree, EVERYWHERE

The Groasis water-box is a clever designed box who feeds a new planted tree exactly enough water to survive its first year and develop roots to become independent. The box also protects the tree against wildlife.

Just Digg It

What happens to rainfall on bare dry soil? The rainwater runs off the bare ground and evaporated. JustDiggIt is a movement who learn locals to catch the rainwater and green there bare ground.

Permaculture

Permaculture is a science for designing the human living environment in a way that is ecologically sustainable and economically stable. It is a way of life. Learn everything about our natural place on the soil.

Liquid nanoclay

“Norwegian scientist invented a process to mix nano-particles of clay with water and bind them to sand particles to condition desert soil. The treatment gives sand particles a clay coating which completely changes their physical properties and allows them to bind with and hold water. It can change any poor-quality sandy soils into high-yield agricultural land in just seven hours.”
